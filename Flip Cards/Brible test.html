Player Improvement Stats

Player Improvement Stats

Rules for applying player improvement stats are as follows. These stats cannot be applied to just any player but must follow these strict guidelines:

  • Transfer Bought Players: Stats can only be applied to players purchased during the save.
  • Current Club Players: Includes players who were already at the club when the manager joined.
  • Players Obtained by Perks:
    • Applies only to players classified as EXTREMELY INTERESTED.

Milestones

Milestones

Positive Milestones

  • Goal of the Month: Spin attribute wheel (+1 to +3 Shooting stats).
  • Penalty Recovery:
    • If available, scoring 2 penalties removes previous lost points.
    • Missed penalty points in final game apply to next season.

Negative Milestones

  • Red Card: Spin negative attribute wheel (opponent selects, 30% chance to avoid points loss). Player gains +1 Aggression.
  • 3 Consecutive Losses: Sack Assistant Manager and best coach (opponents can claim better coaches).
  • Missed Penalty: -1 Penalty Taking attribute and must take the next penalty awarded.
  • Missed Penalty (Shootout): -2 Penalty Taking attribute.

Budget Wheel

Budget Wheel

  • Cost: £25M Transfer Budget per spin.
  • Frequency: The Budget Wheel can only be spun twice per season, and only during transfer windows.
  • Special Rules:
    • Rolling "Spin Again" does not count as a second spin.
    • If "£20M Spin Again" is rolled, it does count as a second spin.
    • Landing on "Spin Jan Wheel" or "Spin Season Wheel" allows you to spin those wheels regardless of the current transfer window and is in addition to your guaranteed spins.

Derby Rules

Derby Rules

  • Winner: +/- 2 to any attribute for Player of the Match.
  • Loser: Best player cannot play the first 45 minutes of the next match.
  • Red Card: Offending player excluded from the next derby.

End of Season Review

End of Season Review

  • Team of the Year Players: Spin for +/- 3 Attributes. Older players reduce age if over 28.
  • Manager of the Year: Spin Attribute Growth Wheel twice.
  • Cup Wins: £15m for major competitions, £5m for minor cups.
  • Rival Finishing Above You: Spin Attribute Decay Wheel (-1 to -3).
  • Top Performers: Attribute growth based on stats (e.g., goals, assists, clean sheets).

First Season Setup

First Season Setup

  • Equal budgets and wages for all players.
  • January wheel spins occur on the 1st of the month.

New Seasons

New Seasons

Transfer Budgets and Wage Budgets are allocated based on player managers' positions in the league (using an F1 format). The lowest-ranked manager gets the best budget, while the highest-ranked manager gets the least. These allocations are in addition to what the board provides.

Position Transfer Budget
1st £20M
2nd £35M
3rd £50M

Note: These positions (1st, 2nd, 3rd) refer to the standings among player managers, not the overall league table. For example, if the highest-ranked player manager is in 3rd place and the lowest is in 7th place, they are considered 1st and 3rd among player managers respectively.
